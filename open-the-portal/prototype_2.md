# Prototype 2

## Goal
The goal will be to get to the end of the level and defeat the remote controller of the forcefield

## Obstacle
Enemies will be the main obstacle in your path that keep you from progressing if you don't know how to deal with them

## Main Ability
Shoot bullets at enemies

## Limitation
The player can only fire x amount of shots in rapid succession before they can shoot anymore and if they hit their limit they may need to wait for a bit
